# LinkedIn Posting Skill

## Constraints
- Aim for 150-300 words
- Professional tone but not corporate
- Paragraph format with line breaks between sections

## Strategy
- Open with a hook sentence that creates curiosity or states a bold take
- Body: tell the story of what you built/learned/shipped
- Include specific details: metrics, file counts, before/after
- Close with a takeaway or question for discussion

## Format
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Use line breaks generously — LinkedIn collapses long posts
- Bullet points work well for technical details
- First line must hook — it shows before "see more"

## Tone
- Peer sharing learnings, not a press release
- Show vulnerability: what was hard, what surprised you
- Technical depth is OK — LinkedIn devs appreciate it

## Avoid
- "I'm thrilled to announce" or similar corporate openers
- Excessive emoji
- Hashtag spam at the bottom
- Humble-bragging
