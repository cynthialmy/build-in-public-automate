# HackerNews Posting Skill

## Constraints
- Title max 80 characters
- Use "Show HN:" prefix for projects you built
- Use "Ask HN:" prefix for questions to the community
- Body text should be concise and technical

## Strategy
- Title is everything — make it specific and interesting
- Lead with what's technically novel or different about your approach
- HN readers are senior engineers: respect their time and intelligence
- Focus on the "how" and "why", not just the "what"

## Format
- Keep body text short (3-5 paragraphs max)
- Link to source code or demo if available (use `url` field)
- Technical details are welcome but be concise

## Title Examples
- Good: "Show HN: I built a CLI that generates social posts from git commits"
- Good: "Ask HN: How do you handle rate limiting across multiple APIs?"
- Bad: "My Amazing New Tool That Will Change Everything"
- Bad: "Check out what I built this weekend!"

## Avoid
- Hyperbole or marketing speak
- Vague titles
- Posts without substance (just a link)
- Dismissing feedback or alternatives
